Autumn Family · Dark Sub-type

Dark Autumn

"Deep, warm, and quietly dramatic."
Warm
Undertone
Dark
Value
Rich
Chroma
Like the last golden light before winter — intense, rich, and effortlessly bold. Dark Autumn sits at the intersection of True Autumn and Dark Winter: the deepest and most dramatic of the Autumn family. Your colouring carries authority without trying; its power is in depth, not brightness. Espresso, forest green, rust, dark camel, burgundy, deep olive, burnt sienna — these colours don't just suit you, they complete you.

Your Features

Eyes
Dark hazel, warm dark brown, dark green, or warm black. Swirling patterns and iris freckles common. High contrast between iris and whites.
Hair
Medium to dark golden brown, dark auburn, dark chocolate, or black-brown. Develops warm golden or red highlights in sun.
Skin
Neutral to neutral-warm. Light to deep range. Golden or bronze undertones. Tans warmly and easily.

Outfit Formula

Value Contrast

Pair deep darks with warm mediums — dark chocolate with camel, forest green with mustard. Your high-contrast features need an outfit that can hold their own.

Dark chocolate · Camel · Mustard

Earth + Accent

Ground in a neutral (chocolate, camel, ivory), then add one Autumn accent — rust, forest, or teal. Three colors maximum.

Plum · Warm cream · Deep gold

Dark Autumn vs. Dark Winter

You're Dark Autumn if…
Gold flatters more than silver. Earthy, warm tones feel natural. Cool or icy colors feel stark rather than crisp. Your palette has an organic, burnished quality.
Dark Winter looks like…
Cool, blue-black pigments with icy clarity. Silver is the better metal. Pure black and white work. The palette is sharp and cool rather than rich and warm.

What to Avoid

Icy pastels

Too light and cool — creates a washed-out effect

Stark white

Swap for ivory, warm cream, or soft ecru

Bright pink / fuchsia

Cool-toned — clashes with your warm undertone

Dusty blue / periwinkle

Too cool and muted — loses your warmth

Cool grey / silver

Replace with warm taupe, camel, or mushroom
